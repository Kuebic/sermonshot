Carousel 1: Spirituality Exists to Grow Your Love

Title : Speaking with angels isn't enough [pattern interrupt]

- Slide 1: Mountaintop poses, tea rituals, fasting, tongues—human beings express spirituality in a thousand impressive ways.
- Slide 2: "Whoever does not love does not know God, because God is love." (1 John 4:8)
- Slide 3: Paul says faith that moves mountains and knowledge of all mysteries gain you nothing without love (1 Corinthians 13:2).
- Slide 4: The more you grow spiritually, the more capacity you have to love—and the reverse holds too.
- Slide 5: So measure every spiritual practice by one question: is it warming up your heart?
- Slide 6: All spiritual pursuits are ultimately for the strengthening of our love.

Carousel 2: Satan Tells the Truth

Title : Why Satan loves telling the truth [curiosity gap]

- Slide 1: In Eden, God warned Adam and Eve that eating the fruit meant they would surely die.
- Slide 2: "You will not certainly die," the serpent said to the woman. (Genesis 3:4)
- Slide 3: Strangely, both were right—God meant spiritual death, the serpent meant physical.
- Slide 4: Satan loves to lie to you while saying nothing but truths, like a tricky lawyer.
- Slide 5: The twist comes at the end, when he tells you what to do: go ahead, eat the fruit.
- Slide 6: Watch the call to action—that's where Satan twists it.

Carousel 3: Shame Won't Reconnect You to God

Title : Still punishing yourself with shame? [self-relevance]

- Slide 1: After eating the fruit, Adam covered himself with fig leaves and hid from God.
- Slide 2: "But the LORD God called to the man, 'Where are you?'" (Genesis 3:9)
- Slide 3: God came looking for an open-hearted conversation, hoping Adam would bring Him his fear and pain.
- Slide 4: Our instinct says staying in the shame longer somehow makes up for what we did.
- Slide 5: Yet no amount of shame could reconnect Adam to God, and none will reconnect you.
- Slide 6: Shame is not part of God's restorative process.
