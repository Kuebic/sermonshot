Carousel 1: Spirituality Exists to Grow Your Love

Title : Move mountains, fathom mysteries, gain nothing. [pattern interrupt]

- Slide 1: Human beings express spirituality in amazing ways: poses on mountaintops, tea rituals, fasting, prayer.
- Slide 2: Paul names the most impressive gifts imaginable: tongues of angels, prophecy, faith that moves mountains.
- Slide 3: "If I have a faith that can move mountains, but do not have love, I am nothing" (1 Corinthians 13:2).
- Slide 4: At the end of the day, God cares about one question: is your heart warming up?
- Slide 5: The more you grow spiritually, the more capacity you have to love, and the reverse holds too.
- Slide 6: All spirituality exists to strengthen your love.

Carousel 2: Separation From God Is a Hardening of the Heart

Title : Feeling far from God? Check your heart. [self-relevance]

- Slide 1: Scripture says people are separated from the life of God through the hardening of their hearts (Ephesians 4:18).
- Slide 2: The distance you feel isn't geography; it's a heart slowly turning to stone.
- Slide 3: "I will remove from you your heart of stone and give you a heart of flesh" (Ezekiel 36:26).
- Slide 4: God's promise is a soft, warm, beating heart, full of love.
- Slide 5: So if you want less separation from God, start by softening your heart toward people.
- Slide 6: Reconnection to God begins where the heart softens.

Carousel 3: Satan Tells Truths but Twists the Call to Action

Title : Satan's favorite lie is the truth. [curiosity gap]

- Slide 1: In the garden, the serpent told Eve something technically true.
- Slide 2: "'You will not certainly die,' the serpent said to the woman" (Genesis 3:4).
- Slide 3: She wouldn't die physically, yet eating the fruit meant spiritual death, a heart cut off from God's life.
- Slide 4: Like a tricky lawyer, Satan loves to deceive you using nothing but accurate facts.
- Slide 5: The twist always comes at the end, in the call to action: so go ahead, disobey God.
- Slide 6: Watch the call to action — that's where the deception lives.
