Excerpt:
We talked about the oneness of God. How in Genesis, well, the connection between Deuteronomy 6. Four, the Lord our God is one, the Lord is one comes from. Has the Hebrew word echad, E, C, H, A, D. This is the same word, one for the oneness of God and one for the oneness of man and wife. And so we can deduce from that that the oneness of God is a harmony. It's not a singular unity. It's a harmony of two becoming one.
