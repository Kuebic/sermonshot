Carousel 1: Hearts Burning on the Emmaus Road

Title : Were not our hearts burning? [curiosity gap]

- Slide 1: After the resurrection, two disciples walked the road with a stranger who opened the Scriptures to them.
- Slide 2: Only when he broke the bread did their eyes open: they had been walking with Jesus.
- Slide 3: "Were not our hearts burning within us while he talked with us on the road and opened the Scriptures to us?" (Luke 24:32)
- Slide 4: Jesus taught the Word with such passion that it left his disciples burning inside.
- Slide 5: The Bereans were called noble because they received the message eagerly and examined the Scriptures every day (Acts 17:11).
- Slide 6: Carrying the fire means the Word lights your path and strengthens you wherever you go.

Carousel 2: Fight the Enemy's Lies with Scripture

Title : Good enough for Jesus, good enough for me [self-relevance]

- Slide 1: When the devil tempted Jesus three times in the wilderness, Jesus answered every time by quoting Scripture.
- Slide 2: "Man shall not live on bread alone, but on every word that comes from the mouth of God" (Matthew 4:4).
- Slide 3: The enemy's main weapon is lies: that you can't grow, can't overcome, that others will never change.
- Slide 4: With the Holy Spirit and an eagerness for the Word, anybody can be transformed and redeemed.
- Slide 5: Even one day of fear-driven thinking can set a course with ramifications that last for years.
- Slide 6: If using Scripture to beat back the enemy is good enough for Jesus, it's good enough for me.

Carousel 3: Echad — Oneness of God, Oneness of Family

Title : God and marriage share one Hebrew word [pattern interrupt]

- Slide 1: When asked the greatest commandment, Jesus opened his answer with the foundational declaration of Judaism, the Shema.
- Slide 2: "Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God, the LORD is one" (Deuteronomy 6:4).
- Slide 3: The Hebrew word for "one" is echad, a oneness with complexity, more than a single thing.
- Slide 4: Genesis 2:24 uses echad again: a man is united to his wife and they become one flesh.
- Slide 5: If we want to see more of God in the world, we need more oneness in our families.
- Slide 6: Study the Word with eagerness and let it embolden you toward unbreakable faith and unbreakable families.
