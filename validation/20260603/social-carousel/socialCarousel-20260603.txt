Carousel 1: Hearts Burning for the Word

Title : Why were their hearts burning? [curiosity gap]

- Slide 1: After the resurrection, two disciples shared a meal with a stranger they couldn't quite place.
- Slide 2: When he broke the bread, their eyes were opened — they recognized Jesus, and he disappeared from their sight.
- Slide 3: "Were not our hearts burning within us... as he opened the Scriptures to us?" (Luke 24:32)
- Slide 4: The Bereans were noble in character because they examined the Scriptures every day with eagerness (Acts 17:11).
- Slide 5: Scripture isn't an egghead exercise; it's spiritual and emotional power to be better at everything you do.
- Slide 6: A burning heart starts with an open Bible.

Carousel 2: Fighting the Enemy's Lies with Scripture

Title : One day of bad thinking costs years [emotion/loss]

- Slide 1: The enemy's main weapon is lies — about you, about others, about whether anyone can really change.
- Slide 2: Decisions made from fear, anxiety, pride, or insecurity can set a course with ramifications for years.
- Slide 3: When Satan tempted Jesus in the wilderness, Jesus answered every lie by quoting Scripture (Matthew 4:1-11).
- Slide 4: If using Scripture to beat back the enemy is good enough for Jesus, it's good enough for me.
- Slide 5: Paul tells us to put on the full armor of God — and it culminates in the Word (Ephesians 6:10-18).
- Slide 6: Anybody can be transformed, anybody can be redeemed — that's the truth that fights the lies.

Carousel 3: The Oneness of God (Echad) and Families

Title : "One" doesn't mean what you think [pattern interrupt]

- Slide 1: "Hear, O Israel: The Lord our God, the Lord is one" (Deuteronomy 6:4) — the Shema, Judaism's foundational declaration.
- Slide 2: Asked the greatest commandment, Jesus opened with that exact sentence, then said love God, love people (Mark 12:29-31).
- Slide 3: The Hebrew word for one is echad — not a singular one like one phone, but a complex oneness.
- Slide 4: Echad appears again in Genesis 2:24, where a man and wife are united and become one flesh.
- Slide 5: If we want to see more of God in the world, we need more oneness in families.
- Slide 6: What is bound on earth is bound in heaven — so build unbreakable faith and unbreakable families (Matthew 16:19).
